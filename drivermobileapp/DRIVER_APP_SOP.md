## 1. Installation & Setup

### Android / Chrome

1. Open the app URL in **Google Chrome**
2. A blue banner appears at the top: **"Install Matanuska App"**
3. Tap **Install App**
4. The app is added to your home screen — open it from there going forward

### iPhone / Safari

1. Open the app URL in **Safari**
2. A blue banner appears: **"Install Matanuska App"**
3. Tap the **Share icon** (↗️) at the bottom of Safari
4. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **Add** to confirm

### Updates

- The app updates automatically in the background
- You may briefly see an **"Updating app…"** screen — this is normal
- No action is required from you

## 2. Logging In

1. Open the app from your home screen
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Tap **Sign In**
4. Wait for the **"Authenticating…"** message (1–3 seconds)
5. You will land on the **Home** page

### Auto-Logout

- The app signs you out after **5 minutes of inactivity** (no tapping, scrolling, or typing)
- Your email is saved — you only need to re-enter your **password**
- This is a security feature and is normal behaviour

### Can't Log In?

- Double-check your email and password
- Tap **Contact Support** on the login screen to reach your fleet administrator

## 3. Home Page

The Home page is your dashboard. It shows at a glance:

### Assigned Vehicles

- Your **truck** (fleet number, registration, make/model)
- Your **reefer** unit (if one is assigned to you)
- If nothing appears, contact your supervisor to get a vehicle assigned

### Document Expiry Banner

- A **red** or **orange** alert appears if any of your documents are expired or expiring within 30 days
- Tap the alert to go to the Documents page

### Monthly Overview

Four stat cards showing your current month totals:

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|---------------|
| **KM Traveled** | Total distance driven |
| **Diesel** | Total litres logged |
| **Trips** | Total trips / completed trips |
| **Fuel Cost** | Total diesel spend (USD) |

Tap **Diesel**, **Trips**, or **Fuel Cost** cards to jump directly to those pages.

### Recent Activity

- Your last **3 trips** and last **2 diesel entries**
- Tap any item to view full details

### Refreshing Data

- **Tap the refresh button** (circular arrow icon) in the top-right corner of the page to reload all data
- **Swipe down** from the top of the page to pull-to-refresh (works on every page)
- **Switching tabs** (Home, Diesel, Trips, Profile) automatically refreshes the data on that page — no manual action needed

## 4. Diesel Logging

Navigate to the **Diesel** page using the bottom navigation bar (droplet icon).

### Viewing Your Diesel History

- Use the **month dropdown** to select a month, or switch to **Custom** for a specific date range
- Each entry shows: date, litres, cost, km reading, distance, fuel station, and consumption (km/L)
- Entries added by the fleet office are marked with a **"Dashboard"** badge

### Debriefing Alert

If any entry shows fuel consumption below **2.0 km/L**, a banner appears:

> **"Debriefing Required — X transaction(s) with abnormal consumption detected. Please visit the office for debriefing."**

### Adding a Diesel Entry

1. Tap the **blue + button** (bottom-right corner)
2. Select the fuel target:
   - **Truck** — for the main vehicle
   - **Reefer** — for the refrigerated unit (only appears if you have a reefer assigned)
3. Fill in **all required fields**:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|:--------:|-------|
| Date | ✅ | Defaults to today; can select a past date |
| Fuel Station | ✅ | Searchable dropdown — type to search (Shell, BP, ACM, etc.) |
| Litres Filled | ✅ | Decimal allowed (e.g. 45.5) |
| Total Cost | ✅ | Amount in USD |
| Km Reading | ✅ | Current vehicle odometer |
| Notes | — | Optional — add any additional details |

4. **Upload photos** (recommended):
   - Tap **Camera** to take a live photo, or **Gallery** to select from your phone
   - Upload your **diesel slip** photo
   - Upload your **pump display** photo
   - Maximum **5 files**, each up to **10 MB**
5. Tap **Save Entry**
6. A success animation confirms the save — you return to the diesel list

## 5. Trip Management

Navigate to the **Trips** page using the bottom navigation bar (route icon).

### Viewing Your Trips

- Filter by **month** or use **Custom** date range
- Each trip card shows: trip number, route (origin → destination), date, status, and distance

### Trip Details

Tap any trip card to open the **Trip Detail Sheet**. It has four tabs:

### Tab 1: Overview

- View trip number, status, dates, client, distance, and revenue
- **Edit** these fields:
  - **Pod Number** — enter the proof-of-delivery number
  - **Starting KM** — odometer at trip start
  - **Ending KM** — odometer at trip end
- Tap **Save** to update

### Tab 2: Cycle Tracker

For produce and agricultural loads, track your trip through 6 phases:

| Phase | What to Log |
|-------|-------------|
| **1. Preparation** | Vehicle inspection times, refuel times, reefer startup temperature |
| **2. Farm Loading** | Farm arrival time, loading start/end times, delay reasons, supervisor name |
| **3. Transit** | Automatically tracked |
| **4. Depot Arrival** | Arrival time, reefer temperature on arrival, offloading times |
| **5. Return** | Crates/bins count, damage details (if any), depot supervisor name |
| **6. Completion** | Yard arrival time, unloading times, road comments |

**How to use:**

1. Tap a phase to open its form
2. Fill in all the required fields
3. Submit — the phase gets a ✅ checkmark
4. Move to the next phase
5. When all phases are done, tap **Mark As Complete**

Phase durations (e.g. "2h 15m for loading") calculate automatically.

### Tab 3: Expenses

View and add expenses linked to this trip. See [Section 6: Expenses](#6-expenses) for details.

### Tab 4: Freight

- View linked cargo details (type, description, weight, status)
- Tap **Link Freight** to attach cargo:
  1. Select **Freight Type** from the dropdown
  2. Enter **Cargo Description**
  3. Enter **Weight** (kg)
  4. Set **Status** (pending / in progress / completed / cancelled)
  5. Tap **Save Freight**
- You can edit or delete linked freight at any time

## 6. Expenses

You can access expenses in two ways:

- **From a trip**: Trips → tap a trip → Expenses tab (expense is linked to that trip)
- **From the Expenses page**: Bottom nav → Profile → or standalone expense entry

### Adding an Expense

1. Tap **Add Expense** (or the + button)
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|:--------:|-------|
| Category | ✅ | Border Costs, Parking, Diesel, Non-Value-Added, Allowances, Tolls |
| Sub-category | ✅ | Auto-populates based on category selection |
| Amount | ✅ | In USD |
| Date | ✅ | Defaults to today |
| Reference # | — | Invoice or receipt number |
| Notes | — | Optional details |
| Receipt photos | — | Up to 5 photos, 10 MB each |

3. Tap **Save Expense**

### Auto-Flagging

Expenses in **high-risk categories** (Border Costs, Non-Value-Added Costs) are automatically flagged for review by management. This is normal — ensure you have a valid receipt uploaded.

## 7. Profile & Documents

Navigate to the **Profile** page using the bottom navigation bar (user icon).

### Profile Page

- Shows your **name**, **avatar**, and **assigned vehicles**
- Menu items:
  - **Notifications** — shows document alert count
  - **Documents** — view your document statuses
  - **Support** — contact your administrator
  - **About** — app version information
- **Sign Out** button at the bottom

### Documents Page

Access via Profile → **Documents**, or by tapping the document expiry banner on the Home page.

Shows 6 document types with expiry status:

| Document | Status Colours |
|----------|----------------|
| Driver's License | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |
| PDP (Professional Driving Permit) | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |
| Passport | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |
| Medical Certificate | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |
| Retest Certificate | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |
| Defensive Driving Certificate | 🟢 Valid / 🟡 Expiring / 🔴 Expired |

**Status meanings:**

- **🟢 Green "Valid"** — more than 30 days until expiry
- **🟡 Orange "X days left"** — expiring within 30 days — renew soon
- **🔴 Red "Expired X days ago"** — past expiry — contact your fleet manager immediately
- **⚪ Grey "No date"** — no expiry date on file

> **Documents are read-only in the app.** Contact your fleet manager to update expired or missing documents.

## 8. Daily Workflows

### Start of Shift

1. Open the app → **Home** page
2. Confirm your **vehicle assignment** is correct
3. Check for **document expiry alerts** — resolve if needed
4. Go to **Trips** → find today's trip
5. Open the trip → **Cycle Tracker** → complete **Phase 1 (Preparation)**

### At a Refuel Stop

1. Go to the **Diesel** page
2. Tap the **+ button**
3. Select **Truck** (or **Reefer**)
4. Enter: date, fuel station, litres, total cost, km reading
5. Take photos of the **diesel slip** and **pump display**
6. Tap **Save Entry**

### Logging a Trip Expense

1. Go to **Trips** → tap the relevant trip
2. Go to the **Expenses** tab
3. Tap **Add Expense**
4. Select category and sub-category
5. Enter amount, reference number, and date
6. Upload a **receipt photo**
7. Tap **Save Expense**

### End of Trip

1. Go to **Trips** → tap the trip
2. **Overview** tab → enter your **Ending KM** → tap **Save**
3. **Cycle Tracker** tab → complete any remaining phases
4. Tap **Mark As Complete** when all phases are done

### End of Shift

1. Review the **Home** page — confirm your stats look correct
2. The app will auto-logout after 5 minutes of inactivity, or tap **Profile → Sign Out**

## 9. Rules & Best Practices

| # | Rule |
|---|------|
| 1 | **Always log km readings** at the start and end of every trip |
| 2 | **Always photograph** your diesel slip and pump display at every refuel |
| 3 | **Upload receipts** for every expense — unreceipted expenses get flagged |
| 4 | **Complete all 6 Cycle Tracker phases** before marking a trip complete |
| 5 | **Check document expiry** regularly — expired documents block operations |
| 6 | **Do not share your login** — activity is tracked per driver account |
| 7 | **Tap the refresh button or swipe down** if data looks outdated — switching tabs also auto-refreshes |
| 8 | **Save entries promptly** — don't wait until end of day to log diesel or expenses |

## 10. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| App won't load | Check your internet connection. Close and reopen the app. |
| "Authenticating…" hangs | Check your internet. Try closing the app and logging in again. |
| Signed out unexpectedly | Normal after 5 minutes of inactivity. Re-enter your password. |
| Data disappeared after switching apps | Tap the **refresh button** (↻) or swipe down on any page. The app auto-recovers your session — your data is not lost. |
| "No Vehicle Assigned" | Contact your supervisor to assign a vehicle to your account. |
| Can't upload photos | Check file size is under 10 MB. Try a different photo. |
| Diesel entry flagged | Consumption below 2.0 km/L — visit the office for debriefing. |
| Document shows "Expired" | Contact your fleet manager to update the document. |
| Data not updating | Tap the **refresh button** (↻) at the top of the page, or swipe down to pull-to-refresh. Switching to another tab and back also refreshes data. Check your internet connection if the problem persists. |
| App looks outdated | Tap the refresh button first. If that doesn't help, close the app completely and reopen it — updates install automatically. |
| Can't find a trip | Change the month filter or use the Custom date range. |
